Uchiha Clan Members

  • Itachi Uchiha
  • Sasuke Uchiha
  • Madara Uchiha
  • Obito Uchiha

Itachi Uchiha

Itachi Uchiha was a prodigy of the Uchiha clan and served as an ANBU Captain at a very young age. He later became a member of the Akatsuki and played a major role in the Fourth Shinobi War. Despite being seen as a villain by many, Itachi sacrificed his own life to protect the Leaf Village and his younger brother, Sasuke.

Sasuke Uchiha

Sasuke Uchiha is the younger brother of Itachi and one of the last surviving members of the Uchiha clan. He was initially portrayed as a rival to Naruto Uzumaki, but later joined forces with him to save the world. Sasuke is known for his Sharingan and later for his Rinnegan, which he obtained from the Sage of Six Paths.

Madara Uchiha

Madara Uchiha was one of the strongest members of the Uchiha clan and a co-founder of Konoha. He later defected from the village and became one of the main antagonists of the Naruto series. Madara was known for his Eternal Mangekyo Sharingan and later for his Rinnegan.

Obito Uchiha

Obito Uchiha was a member of the Uchiha clan and a former student of Kakashi Hatake. He later became a member of the Akatsuki and played a major role in the Fourth Shinobi War. Obito was known for his Sharingan and later for his Six Paths of Pain technique.
